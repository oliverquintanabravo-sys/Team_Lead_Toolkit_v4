About this guide

Built by a Team Lead, for the next one

This is an internal resource for the Center's Expanded Learning program. It pulls the practical parts of the Intern to Team Lead workshop — work plans, enrichment, structured play, communication, and operations — into something you can reach for in 30 seconds when you're standing in your classroom and need an answer. Nothing here replaces your Site Coordinator; it just means you're never starting from a blank page.
